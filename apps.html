About This App

HC Central is a professional business management application built specifically for home care and maintenance service companies. Designed by HC Care Technologies, the app empowers supervisors and field technicians to manage service calls end-to-end — from initial assignment to final resolution — all from a single, intuitive mobile interface. The platform features a smart urgency color system that helps teams prioritize critical tasks instantly. Technicians can upload job completion photos, supervisors can monitor all active calls in real time, and the entire workflow is streamlined to reduce delays and improve customer satisfaction. Whether you are managing a small team or a large fleet of technicians, HC Central scales with your operation and delivers consistent, trackable results every single time.

Key Features
Service Call Assignment
Supervisors can create and assign service calls to the right technicians instantly with full job details attached.
Urgency Color System
Visual priority indicators — red for critical, amber for pending, green for completed — make triage effortless at a glance.
Technician Photo Uploads
Field technicians can capture and upload job completion photos directly within the app for verified proof of service.
Supervisor Monitoring
A dedicated supervisor dashboard provides a full real-time overview of all ongoing, completed, and pending service calls.
Real-Time Tracking
Track technician status and job progress in real time, ensuring no call goes unattended or falls through the cracks.
End-to-End Workflow
From service request to job closure, every step is tracked, logged, and verifiable — keeping your team fully accountable.
